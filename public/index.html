Smart Ration Shop System

A complete web portal for ration beneficiaries, shop sellers, administrators, and delivery staff. Place monthly ration orders, maintain stock in real time, and monitor operations from one unified dashboard.

User Portal

Beneficiaries can log in, view available stock for their linked shop, place new orders, and track past transactions.

Open User Login

Seller Portal

Shop sellers can update current stock levels, review order flow, and monitor distribution at shop level.

Open Seller Login

Admin Panel

Administrators can review total transactions, identify low stock shops, and inspect system-wide performance.

Open Admin Login

Delivery Portal

Delivery staff can log in by village and phone, then view monthly home delivery assignments.
